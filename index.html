More on public charge
Benefits currently considered for public charge include:

  • SSI
  • General Assistance
  • CalWORKS
  • CAPI
Additionally, long-term Medi-Cal care such as nursing homes will be considered.
Proposed changes to public charge will likely consider:
  • CalFresh/food stamps
  • Non-emergency Medi-Cal
  • Federal housing/Section 8

These changes might never be made. If they are, you will have time to stop these benefits before they are in place.
