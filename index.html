If a public charge test applies, only a few benefits that an applicant receives would count. It is safe to get CalFresh/SNAP and any Medi-Cal/Medicaid benefits until 10/15/2019.

Only these benefits are currently considered for public charge:
  • Cash Benefits

  • General Assistance A welfare program that benefit adults without dependents.
